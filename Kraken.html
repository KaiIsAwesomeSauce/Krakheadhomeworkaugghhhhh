Deep Dive Into The Kraken


Welcome

For centuries, people have told stories about a colossal squid roaming the ocean depths, waiting for and attacking sailors who roam near its territory.


The kraken is probably one of the most well known and oldest cryptids in history, with sightings of it dating back to 1100 BC. According to the legends, the Kraken is an oversized creature, commonly depicted as an octopus or squid, that's highly aggressive and will wreck any ship it comes across.


This website will be dedicated to thoroughly analyzing the creature of myths.

Erik Pontoppidan proposed that since the kraken ate a lot of fish, its feces would, allegedly, smell delectable. This would lure other fish towards it and the kraken would eat them as well. Rinse and repeat.
